About Us

Bringing Expert Healthcare to Azamgarh

Phoolpur Multispeciality Hospital is a unit of The Phoolpur Educational Welfare Trust, established with one singular mission — to bring world-class medical care to the heart of Azamgarh district so that people no longer need to travel far from home for expert treatment.

Our hospital is led by highly qualified doctors from leading institutions in Delhi and Lucknow, who have returned to serve their community with the highest standards of medicine and genuine human compassion.

We offer state-of-the-art surgical facilities, a modern computerised pathology lab, 24/7 emergency services, ambulance support and specialist consultations — all under one roof.

Patient safety is our highest priority
Compassionate, patient-centred care
Advanced modern surgical technology
Serving our community with dedication
